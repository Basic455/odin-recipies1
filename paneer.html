Paneer

Description

Indian Cottage Cheese, can be made into curry, can be roasted and can be eaten raw

Ingridients

  • milk
  • vineger or lime juice

Steps

  1. Boil the cream milk until lukewarm
  2. Mix a table spoon of vineger or lime juice
  3. Stir until the milk is curdled and solids are seperating from the water
  4. Seperate the milk solids and wash them if too sour
  5. Paneer is ready
